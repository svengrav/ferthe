> [!SUMMARY]
> ferthe is a location-based app for intentional exploration of your surroundings. Users discover spots along curated trails, document discoveries with photos and comments, and share insights with a global community. The app remembers your progress across devices and surfaces discoveries through likes and dislikes. Built by a small independent team in Münster with a focus on privacy, local storage, and free usage without registration.

Somewhere in **Münster**, in the heart of Europe, someone discovers a place.
Like a fox, ferthe moves with intent, curious, quiet, and always tracking the untold story...
Always on the trail.

**ferthe** follows that spirit, inviting you to rediscover the world around you.
Spots tell stories. You collect them, explore connections, and find your own path.

Thoughtfully created in **Münster** – where history, quality, and independence meet.

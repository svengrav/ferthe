# Stumble

The stumble feature is a core part of the discovery experience on ferthe. It allows users to share and discover new places in their city by sharing their "stumbles" places they've visited and found interesting. 

A stumble consists of a place (a point of interest, or POI) and a short description. Users can share their stumbles as Spots, which are then visible to other users in the app.

Concept: As the app starts growing not every location has already spots, so it encourages users to share their stumbles and discover new places. The stumble feature is designed to be simple and easy to use, allowing users to quickly share their spots and discover new places in their city.


## Categories

- The user can choose different categories he wants POI suggestions for. This allows users to discover new places that are relevant to their interests and preferences. For example, a user interested in food can choose the "Food" category to see stumbles related to restaurants, cafes, and other food-related places.

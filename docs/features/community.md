# Community

## Definition

- A Community is a social space centered around a trail.
- Members share discoveries within the community.
- Stats are based on shared discoveries only.

## Sharing Rules

- A discovery is private by default.
- It can be shared with a community within 24 hours of creation.
- The spot must belong to the community's trail.
- A discovery can currently be shared in one community.

## Structure

- Each community has one trail (multiple trails planned for later).
- Members can view shared discoveries from other members.
- Community has a creator and a list of members.

--- 

## Futures

- Multiple trails per community.
- Push notification when someone shares a discovery in a community you are part of.
- Community members' discoveries visible as markers on the map.
